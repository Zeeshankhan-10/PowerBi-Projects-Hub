# ⚽ Premier League Performance Dashboard (Power BI Project)

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing team and player performance in the English Premier League. It provides insights into key football metrics such as goals, assists, tackles, cards, and overall team performance.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Analyze team performance across key football metrics  
- Compare teams based on goals, ratings, and match statistics  
- Track disciplinary records (yellow/red cards)  
- Evaluate player contributions such as assists and defensive actions

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Data Visualization  

---

## 📊 Dataset
The dataset includes football performance data such as:
- Team names (Arsenal, Manchester City, etc.)  
- Goals scored  
- Assists  
- Tackles and saves  
- Clean sheets  
- Yellow and red cards  
- Penalty and free-kick goals  
- Player/team ratings  
- Team of the Week appearances  

---

## 🔄 Process
- Cleaned and structured raw football data using Power Query  
- Built calculated measures for key KPIs  
- Designed interactive visuals to compare team performance  
- Implemented filters to allow team-level analysis  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- **KPI Cards**:
  - Tackles (10K)  
  - Saves (2K)  
  - Own Goals (34)  
  - Penalty Goals (66)  
  - Red Cards (39)  
  - Yellow Cards (1K)  
  - Free Kick Goals (16)  
  - Assists (551)  
  - Clean Sheets (1K)  
  - Total Ratings (3.48K)  

- **Goals Scored by Teams** (pie chart)  
- **Ratings by Teams** (donut chart)  
- **Team of the Week Appearances** (line chart)  
- **Average Duels Won & Dribbles Completed** (gauge visuals)  
- Team filter for dynamic analysis  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Top teams consistently dominate in goals scored and overall ratings  
- Defensive metrics such as tackles and clean sheets play a key role in performance  
- Disciplinary actions (yellow/red cards) vary significantly across teams  
- Team of the Week appearances highlight consistently high-performing teams  
- Balance between offensive and defensive metrics impacts overall team success  

---

## ✅ Conclusion
This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of Premier League team performance, combining offensive, defensive, and disciplinary metrics. It enables users to compare teams effectively and gain insights into factors driving success in the league.
