# 👟 Nike Shoe Sales Dashboard (Power BI Project)

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Nike shoe sales performance across regions, product categories, and customer segments. The dashboard provides insights into revenue, profitability, and sales distribution to support business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Analyze sales performance across regions and product lines  
- Monitor profitability and discount impact  
- Understand customer segments (Kids, Men, Women)  
- Evaluate sales distribution across channels

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Data Visualization  

---

## 📊 Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Product Name (Air Force 1, Air Jordan, etc.)  
- Product Categories (Basketball, Running, Lifestyle, etc.)  
- Size and Filters  
- Region (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, etc.)  
- Sales Channel (Online vs Retail)  
- Units Sold  
- Revenue  
- Discount Applied  
- Profit / Loss  

---

## 🔄 Process
- Cleaned and transformed raw sales data using Power Query  
- Built relationships between product, region, and sales data  
- Created calculated measures (profit, revenue, discount averages)  
- Designed an interactive dashboard with filters and visuals  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- **Region-wise Sales Distribution** (treemap)  
- **Profit/Loss Gauge** showing overall profitability (2500)  
- **Average Discount Applied** (0.21)  
- **Product Distribution by Gender Category** (stacked bar chart)  
- **Units Sold by Segment** (Kids, Men, Women)  
- **Revenue Distribution by Sales Channel** (Online vs Retail pie chart)  
- Filters for product name and size  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Sales are distributed across multiple regions, with some regions contributing more significantly  
- Overall profitability is positive, indicating strong business performance  
- Discounts are applied moderately (average ~0.21), impacting revenue margins  
- Sales distribution across Kids, Men, and Women segments is relatively balanced  
- Revenue is almost evenly split between online and retail channels  

---

## ✅ Conclusion
This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales and profitability for Nike products. It helps stakeholders understand regional performance, customer segmentation, and the impact of discounts, enabling more informed business decisions.
